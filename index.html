About us

We are a NGO working towards the betterment of Senior Citizens by helping them with financial aid, health services. We try to make our country a better place for them. We also motivate them to become self-sufficient by helping them in their ventures if tehy want to start one.

History

The NGO was started in September,2020 by a bunch of young college students who had to do something good for the society.
